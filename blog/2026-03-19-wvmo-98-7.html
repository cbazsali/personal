2026-03-19 WVMO 98.7

Brother Mine: Last week I was tuned into a local radio station, WVMO 98.7 in Monona, listening to a radio show co-hosted by a friend of mine, Kevin. Earlier in the day we were texting about an obscure Dylan track he just discovered and was really enjoying. Later, he played it on the radio show and gave me a shoutout regarding our conversation. (And generously made a plug for our upcoming Dylan concert.)
