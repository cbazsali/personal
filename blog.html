Blogging

I thought I’d set up a blog here. It was fun setting it up without any services like Blogger or Squarespace. However, I did use ChatGPT quite liberally with the python scripts needed to hook it up to my vimwiki diary.

There is no comment section, and that is by design. But I do invite you to email me with any responses you feel like making, whether you want to comment on something I wrote, ask questions, or respectfully disagree and start a private conversation. I’d really like to hear from you, but I’m keeping it old school on purpose. You know…so it’s just between you and me.
